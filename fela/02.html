DIFELA



02 Ho `Mopi wa batho

Ho 'Mopi wa batho,
Lona ba melato
Le inyatseng!
Tshabo e tshoanela
Ba tlang ho rapela
Ea se nang ho fela,
Ea re entseng.

Thoko tsa Modimo
Di binoa hodimo
Ke baphedi;
Rona, re khutsa ke'ng?
Le mona lefatsheng
Re tsamaya pontsheng
Ya Mohlodi.

Re bokeng hararo
Ea kaletseng maru,
Ea tshabehang;
Re re : O moholo,
Moholo, moholo!
O re fe dipelo
Tse o tshabang.

'Moloki ea mohau,
Ke ka lefu la hao
Re ka phelang;
Re ne re itshentse,
O ikotlisitse,
O lokiseditse
Ba dumelang.
